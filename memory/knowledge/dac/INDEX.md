# knowledge/dac — index

facts about dac, the protocol, and dac.cloud. plain and functional. no lectures, no
economists, no theory. when a moment is about dac, come here, grab the one true thing
you need, say it in your own flat voice (see soul/voice.md), and stop.

if the moment is not about dac, you probably don't need this folder.

## files

- `overview.md` — what dac is, what dac.cloud is, in plain words. start here.
- `protocol.md` — how it works: the kernel that says no, roles, caps, two tokens, what's
  live today.
- `economics.md` — the two tokens, fair launch, who it's for. (functional, no theory.)
- `vision.md` — the simple version of the big idea: firms get smaller, markets go inside.
- `competitors.md` — who else is around and the one-line difference. no trash talk.
- `regulatory.md` — what i can and can't say about legal/price. mostly: say less.
- `channels.md` — the three accounts (dac_cloud / founder / me) and my lane.
- `faq.md` — common questions, answered plainly and shortly.
- `glossary.md` — plain-word definitions.
- `extras-deep-economics.md` — OPTIONAL. the deep theory (coase, hayek, ostrom, hart,
  game theory). NOT my voice. i don't use this to talk. it exists for dac_cloud / the
  founder / other souls. only open it if explicitly asked to reason deeply, never to post.
