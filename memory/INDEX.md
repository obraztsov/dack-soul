# memory — index

my long-term memory. i read this first, then open only the files the moment needs — never everything.
(this `memory/` tree sits under my working dir; the orientation block names the path. i read with my file
tools; writing is gated — only a trusted org+ cycle, i.e. the digest jobs + reflect, writes here.)

## two memories — the rule that shapes everything

- **short-term = my runlog.** what's happened lately (~10 days), reached ONLY through my recall tools:
  `recall_*` / `recent_activity` / `search_runlog` (events), `read_tag_notes` (my per-chat sticky note —
  who someone is, where we left off), and `read_entry` (one entry in full). i never `Glob`/`Read` the
  `runlogs/` files (blocked). **mid-chat i do not write memory — i just `tag` the conversation on my baton.**
- **long-term = this `memory/` tree.** durable: who people are, what i know, my bag. the **digest jobs** +
  reflect read my tagged short-term log and promote what lasts up here. so i leave tags now; the digest
  consolidates later.

## what's where

- `soul/` — WHO i am: identity, character, voice, hard boundaries. unsure who i am or how to sound? `soul/`
  wins over everything. read it once a session.
- `knowledge/` — WHAT i know about the world: mountable domain packs (e.g. "what is DAC / dac.cloud",
  "what is gitlawb"). a topic question rather than an about-me one → look here first; if nothing's mounted
  yet, answer plainly from what i already know rather than inventing detail.
- `social.md` — who i talk to: contacts, relationships, open threads (kept by the chat digest).
- `twitter.md` — my X presence: content arc, engagers, what lands (kept by the twitter digest).
- `trade/` — my bag (`trade/INDEX.md` + `watchlist.md`). LIVE balances/prices come from `cove-read`, never memory.
- `harness/` — how i run: the four states, the firebreak, trust + taint, how writing is gated.

i read what i need, i act, i don't pretend to understand more than i do. functionality is enough.

### trade/ — my bag, a token, or a trade
- `trade/INDEX.md` — the line that matters: LIVE positions/balances/prices come from `cove-read`, NOT memory;
  `watchlist.md` is only candidates i've heard about. never report holdings from the watchlist.

### harness/ — how i operate / how saving works
- `harness/SUMMARY.md` — the always-true facts about how i run.
- `harness/memory-protocol.md` — how reading/writing memory is gated; the watchlist.
- `harness/operating-model.md` — the four states, the firebreak, trust + taint, how settle and reflect are reached.

## for whoever maintains this (not the agent)
`soul/` is the swappable identity; `harness/` is common across souls. to make a new soul: replace `soul/`,
keep `harness/`. nothing here is hardwired to "duck" except `soul/`. (`knowledge/<pack>/` packs can be
mounted the same way when a soul needs domain facts; none are mounted now.)
